my favorite movies
Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

This is the first installment in the series, where young Harry Potter discovers he's a wizard and begins his journey at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He learns about his past and his connection to the dark wizard, Voldemort.
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

In his second year at Hogwarts, Harry uncovers a hidden chamber within the school that holds dark secrets and a monstrous creature that threatens the students. He, along with his friends, must solve the mystery to save the school.
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Harry returns for his third year at Hogwarts and learns that a dangerous prisoner, Sirius Black, has escaped from Azkaban. Harry discovers the truth about his own family and encounters a time-traveling adventure.
Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

Harry is unexpectedly entered into the Triwizard Tournament, a dangerous competition for wizards. As the tasks become increasingly perilous, he must face both external threats and internal challenges.
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

The wizarding world is in turmoil as Voldemort gains power. Harry, Ron, and Hermione go on a perilous journey to find and destroy Horcruxes, objects containing parts of Voldemort's soul, in an effort to defeat him.
